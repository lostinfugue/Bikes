DC Capital Bikeshare
Analysis of historical data captured at bikeshare stations.

Included are the following directories and files:

-Source: 
* readAllData.R processes the raw data and compiles the separate files into one set containing the number of daily visits for each date and station over the time period.

-Data: 
* Fourteen (14) .csv files for 2010q4-2014q1, raw data from capital share website:
http://www.capitalbikeshare.com/system-data taken on 13 May 2014.

-Analyses: 
* 14 May 2014: Some preliminary analysis.
